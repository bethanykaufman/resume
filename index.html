How I Work

Strategy first. Accountability always.

A few things that define how I lead — and what distinguishes my approach from the norm at director level.

01
I own the outcome, not the activity
Channel tactics are a means to an end. I stay anchored to business outcomes — traffic, acquisition, retention, revenue — and adjust channels and investment accordingly. The media plan serves the goal, not the other way around.
02
I prove what's working before scaling it
I've built dark testing and incrementality methodology into how I run media — not just A/B tests, but real proof that spend drives revenue. That kind of rigor builds internal credibility and protects budgets when business conditions shift.
03
I hold agencies to a real standard
I've run full RFP processes, rebuilt agency relationships, and restructured scopes when work wasn't meeting the mark. I don't manage agencies passively — I set the bar, provide the brief, and measure what matters.
04
I stay ahead of where search is going
Most marketing teams are still catching up to GEO and AI-influenced search. I've already built enterprise-level strategy for both — because brand discoverability in AI results is the next battleground, and waiting is not a strategy.
